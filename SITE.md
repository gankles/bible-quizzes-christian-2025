# Bible Quiz Website - Site Documentation

**Project ID:** bible-quiz-templates-2025
**Stitch Project:** Bible Quiz Website Templates

---

## 1. Site Vision

Create a comprehensive Bible study platform with quiz functionality, lexicon tools, character studies, and verse-by-verse analysis. The site should feel scholarly yet accessible, with modern design patterns and clear navigation.

---

## 2. Target Audience

- Bible students and scholars
- Sunday school teachers
- Personal Bible study users
- Seminary students
- Church small groups

---

## 3. Core Features

1. **Bible Quizzes** - Chapter and book-level quizzes with multiple difficulty levels
2. **Lexicon Tools** - Greek and Hebrew word studies with Strong's concordance
3. **Character Studies** - Biblical figure biographies and timelines
4. **Verse Analysis** - Cross-references, commentary, and original language
5. **Topic Studies** - Thematic exploration across scripture
